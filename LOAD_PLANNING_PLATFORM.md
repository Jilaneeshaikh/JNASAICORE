# Enterprise Load Planning & Logistics Intelligence Platform

The **Enterprise Load Planning & Logistics Intelligence Platform** is a specialized engineering planning system designed to bridge the gap between mechanical packaging designs, intermodal transport configurations, and warehouse storage allocations. 

This platform serves as the structural schema and registry backbone that prepares finished packaging for logistics, fulfillment, and transportation without acting as an operational Transport Management System (TMS) or Warehouse Management System (WMS).

## Architectural Foundations
1. **Packaging-Centric Planning**: Every logistics plan is linked to a verified `PackagingDesign` with resolved dimensional envelopes and Bills of Materials (BOM).
2. **Standardized Equipment**: Restricts container types to standard ISO configurations (20ft, 40ft, High Cube) and industry pallets (GMA) to prevent mechanical stacking incompatibilities.
3. **Structured AI Context Fabric**: Exposes detailed XML context to the AI workspace, enabling seamless copilot consultations on ASTM and ISTA standards.
4. **Comprehensive Revision History**: Logs each packaging configuration and container assignment state for non-repudiation and audits.
