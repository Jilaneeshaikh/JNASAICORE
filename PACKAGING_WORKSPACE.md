# JNAS Packaging Workspace

The **JNAS Packaging Workspace** is a high-density, bento-grid styled industrial cockpit tailored for high-volume logistics and quality assurance management.

## Key Sub-Panels
1. **Faceted Filter Header**:
   - Filter specs by category (e.g. Rack, Pallet, Tote, Dunnage, Corrugated).
   - Filter specs by status (e.g. Draft, Submitted, In Review, Active, Approved).
   - Sync active CAD blueprint contexts instantly.
2. **Material Compound Catalog**:
   - Browse density, tensile strengths, ESD ratings, and aerospace certification states.
3. **IAM Sandbox Isolation Controls**:
   - Transition roles (`Packaging Engineer`, `Engineering Manager`, `Quality Inspector`) to simulate gated approvals and strict logging.
