# Container Registry Specifications

The **Container Registry** maintains standard mechanical, volume, and weight tolerances for transportation equipment, pallets, and stacking racks used to contain physical packaging payloads.

## Standard Equipment Specifications

### 1. 20ft Dry Cargo Container (`ISO-20GP`)
- **Length**: 5,898 mm
- **Width**: 2,352 mm
- **Height**: 2,393 mm
- **Max Weight Capacity**: 21,770 kg
- **Max Volumetric Cube**: 33.2 CBM

### 2. 40ft Dry Cargo Container (`ISO-40GP`)
- **Length**: 12,032 mm
- **Width**: 2,352 mm
- **Height**: 2,393 mm
- **Max Weight Capacity**: 26,780 kg
- **Max Volumetric Cube**: 67.7 CBM

### 3. 40HC High-Cube Container (`ISO-40HC`)
- **Length**: 12,032 mm
- **Width**: 2,352 mm
- **Height**: 2,698 mm
- **Max Weight Capacity**: 26,512 kg
- **Max Volumetric Cube**: 76.2 CBM

### 4. GMA Standard Wooden Pallet (`GMA-PAL-01`)
- **Length**: 1,219 mm
- **Width**: 1,016 mm
- **Height**: 140 mm
- **Max Weight Capacity**: 2,100 kg
- **Max Volumetric Cube**: 1.8 CBM

## Custom Equipment Subsystem
The registry supports custom mechanical containers defined dynamically by the logistics engineers, validating physical dimensions against standard ISO shipping margins.
